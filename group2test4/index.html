Visit the Venues

An introduction to Manchester nightlife.

Nights out in Manchester are wild. The venues vary from genre to genre, or often have a mix. Hence the reason for this guide, to help you plan your nights out!

For example, the Indie club 42's is regularly attended throughout the week and weekends for its dark and cheap, typically studenty atmosphere. Alternatively, Satan's Hollow for the more grungy youth amongst us.

If your taste isn’t for clubbing, the pubs and bars of Manchester may be of interest to you. Manchester Metropolitan University's Union bar is lively on Wednesdays for sports socials, before heading down to Fallowfield for 256 bar and pubs like the Friendship Inn.

An interesting aspect of the city is its range of game based bars such as Junkyard Golf, accompanied by wild cocktails and other beverages. Venues like this are a great start to your night out to liven things up.
