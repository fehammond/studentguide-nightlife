FAQ

Find a frequently asked question and hopefully you find the answer appropriate.

For my own safetey, what areas of Manchester should I probably avoid late at night?

If you're in the city center, you should stay in a group of 3 or more. Areas with higher crime rates aree: Castlefield, Piccadilly Gardens, Gay District and Northen-Quarter. Anywhere outside of the city center its best to get public transport for london.

Which transport serviece should I use and does that change depending on the time of day?

In the early evening you should travel by public transport as it is cheap and reliable. At night its best to get a taxi/Uber from as neer to the venue as possible because trying to navigate the city while intoxicated leaves you vulnerable.

How do I find out if a venue offers student discount?

Check their website or simple ask on the night. Dont be suprised if you get an incorect answer from the internet as certain nights have differnt policys.

What areas of the city center typicaly avoid a lot of tourists?

The arndale and corn exchange are often mobbed with tourists and non-locals even the the area is typical and boring. The better option would be the Northern Quarter and Ancoats because of its large selection of cool and independantly run shops.

Are the nightclubs typicaly accessable for whealchair users?

There are a large number of venues around Manchester that are accessable to wheelchair users but most are not so if you are looking for that type of serviece please do you're research before you decide to go out.
