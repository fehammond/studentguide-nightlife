Mission Statement

Our Manchester is a site guide for new students attending Manchester Metropolitan University. The focus is on city nightlife in Manchester, showing newcomers the most popular venues to attend and upcoming events. We aim to separate ourselves from other sites offering similar services due to our research being from a student point of view, with perks such as the ability for new artists to list their event on our site, and a page dedicated to safety on nights out.
